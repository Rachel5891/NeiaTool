NEIA Pilot Study - Informed Consent

Purpose of the Study

The purpose of this pilot study is to evaluate the effectiveness and accuracy of a newly developed instrument designed to validate NCLEX questions. This will involve comparing your assessment scores with those generated by a custom artificial intelligence (AI) scoring model.

What Does the Study Involve?

  • Use the instrument to assess a set of NCLEX questions.
  • Provide scores based on the criteria outlined in the instrument.
  • Allow your scores to be compared to those generated by our custom AI model to assess the instrument's reliability and validity.

Your participation is voluntary, and you may withdraw at any point. There may be no direct benefit to you from participating in this study; however, your contributions will be valuable in enhancing the validity and reliability of an instrument for nursing education and assessment.

Confidentiality & Risks

There are minimal risks, including the loss of confidentiality. Every effort will be made to maintain confidentiality. Your de-identified information may be shared with other researchers without your additional informed consent.
